One-line summary:
“At every index, make a choice (take / not take), and explore all possibilities.”       

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

🔹 Core Idea:

👉 Har element ke liye 2 choices hoti hain: Include karo | Skip karo
👉 Real Logic:
> Tum array ke har index pe decision lete ho
> Isse ek binary tree ban jata hai

🔹 Flow:
> For index i:
> Take nums[i] → aage badho
> Don’t take nums[i] → aage badho
> Jab end pe pahunch jao (i == n) → jo bana hai (temp) usko answer me daal do

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

❌ temp aur ans same kaam nahi karte
✔️ Dono alag purpose ke liye hain

🔹 Role:

1. temp → current subset (working state)

> Abhi jo bana rahe ho
> Har recursion me change hota hai
> Push / pop hota hai (backtracking)
👉 Example: [1] → [1,2] → [1] → []

2. ans → final result (storage)

> Sare subsets yahan store hote hain
> Ek baar push hua → change nahi hota
👉 Example:
[]
[1]
[1,2]
[2]

🔥 Core logic
Jab base case aata hai: ans.push_back(temp);
Matlab: “jo abhi bana hai (temp) usko save kar lo”
